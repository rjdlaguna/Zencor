ABOUT ZENCOR AGRICULTURAL SOLUTIONS

Founded in 2015, Zencor has been dedicated to helping farmers grow healthy, productive crops with our range of high-quality agricultural products. We specialize in sustainable farming practices and innovative solutions that protect fields from pests and disease.

What started as a simple goal to provide reliable insecticides and herbicides has grown into a trusted name in the Philippine agricultural industry. From onion herbicides to specialized fertilizers, we support farmers through every stage of the growing process to ensure food security and community growth.

OUR MISSION

We want to make farming easier and more sustainable. By offering products that keep crops safe and help them grow strong, we aim to be a trusted partner for farmers everywhere.

OUR VISION

We envision a future where farming is safe, sustainable, and thriving for all. By empowering farmers with innovative crop protection, we preserve the environment and ensure food security for generations to come.

OUR CORE VALUES

Trust & Reliability

We build lasting relationships by offering reliable crop care guidance and products that farmers can depend on for every harvest.

Sustainability

We commit to eco-friendly agricultural practicesthat protect the environment while supporting long-term farming success in the Philippines.

Agricultural Innovation

We continuously improve our crop protection tools to make farming more efficient, resilient, and profitable for the modern grower.

Zencor is committed to making farming easier, safer, and more sustainable. Since 2015, we've partnered with farmers by providing trusted crop care solutions — from protection to nourishment — that empower communities and ensure harvests for generations to come.
