How We Support Filipino Farmers

Comprehensive **crop protection solutions** and agricultural support designed to help farmers achieve healthier, higher-yielding harvests.

HERBICIDES & WEED MANAGEMENT

Our herbicides are the best solutions for onion farming and rice fields, targeting invasive weeds effectively. By removing competition for nutrients, they allow your crops to thrive and maximize yield potential safely.

INSECTICIDES & PEST CONTROL

Protect your fields from harmful pests with our fast-acting crop protection products. Designed to safeguard leaves and roots from damage, our insecticides ensure consistent plant health and stronger seasonal yields.

FUNGICIDES & DISEASE PREVENTION

Defend your crops against rice blast disease and other fungal infections. Our fungicides prevent diseases that weaken harvests, ensuring your plants remain resilient, productive, and green throughout the season.

FERTILIZERS & NUTRIENT CARE

Boost growth with essential nutrients from our foliar fertilizers. Paired with our molluscicides and sprayers, these agricultural solutions provide the tools needed to nourish and protect harvests with confidence.
