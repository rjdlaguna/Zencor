Coden and Flory: Onion Herbicide Solutions Trusted by Filipino Farmers

Weed competition is one of the major challenges in onion production. Zencor offers reliable herbicide solutions such as Coden and Flory, which are commonly used by Filipino farmers to manage weeds during critical growth stages of onion crops.

Coden herbicide is effective in controlling broadleaf weeds and grasses that compete with onions for nutrients, water, and sunlight. It helps maintain clean fields, allowing onion plants to develop stronger roots and uniform bulb formation.

Flory herbicide supports early weed control and is designed to reduce weed pressure during the early stages of onion growth. Proper application of Flory helps minimize crop stress and improves overall field management.

When used according to label instructions and good agricultural practices, Coden and Flory contribute to improved onion yield, better crop quality, and more efficient farm operations.
